How it works

Three steps. About a minute. Nothing to install.

  1. 1

    Connect your wallet

    Open the launcher and pick any Solana wallet — Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, or anything speaking the Wallet Standard.

  2. 2

    Claim your username

    Play

    You land straight in the world, in your browser tab. Build, mine, run jobs, and earn MUCHU while you play.

One currency, on both sides of the screen. The coins in your in-game pocket and the SPL token in your wallet are the same thing, exchanged 1:1.

Earn
Join a job — mining, farming, fishing and more — and every block you break or crop you harvest pays you MUCHU in-game.
Withdraw
Cash out any time. Withdrawals send real MUCHU on-chain from the server treasury to the same wallet that owns your username — never anywhere else.

Heads up: the beta economy runs on Solana devnet — devnet MUCHU is play money with no monetary value. A mainnet cutover is planned; balances and usernames carry over.

Questions, answered

Is this real Minecraft?

Yes. The server is a genuine Paper (Java Edition) server running real plugins, and the browser client speaks the actual Minecraft protocol — it is the open-source minecraft-web-client project, self-hosted here. You share the world with everyone else on the server. MuchuCraft is a community project, not affiliated with Mojang or Microsoft.

What wallet do I need?

Any Solana wallet that can sign a message: Phantom, Solflare, Backpack and most others work out of the box. Signing in never sends a transaction and never costs fees — a signature is all it takes.

Is my username mine forever?

The first wallet to claim a username owns it — the binding is permanent and only that wallet can play under the name. Nobody, including us, can sign in as you without your wallet.

What does depositing do?

Two things. Every deposit from your bound wallet is credited to your in-game balance 1:1, and once your lifetime deposits cross the unlock threshold you get access to every job at full earning speed — forever. You can withdraw your balance back on-chain at any time, so nothing is locked away.
