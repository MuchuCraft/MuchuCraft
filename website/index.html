Questions

Is this actually Minecraft?

Yes — a real Paper server with real plugins. The browser client is the open-source project behind mcraft.fun and speaks the genuine Minecraft protocol. Java Edition survival, not a remake.

What does signing cost?

Nothing. A signature is a proof, not a transaction — no fees, no approvals, and the server never touches your funds.

Can someone take my username?

No. The name is bound to your wallet, and the server kills any connection that tries to use your name without your session. Losing the wallet means losing the name, so keep it safe.

Is my stuff safe?

Spawn is fully protected. In the wild, claim land with a golden shovel — claimed blocks can't be broken by anyone else. You start with 200 claim blocks and accrue more every hour.

Can I really cash out?

Yes — withdrawals send real tokens to the wallet that owns your name, and only that wallet. It's on devnet today, so treat MUCHU as arcade money until mainnet.
